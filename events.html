OUR UPCOMING EVENTS

Our event website serves as the gateway to a multitude of exciting experiences, where cutting-edge ideas and intellectual curiosity converge. Explore a dynamic calendar of events that showcase the prowess of our students, faculty, and esteemed guest speakers. From hackathons that push the boundaries of creativity to insightful lectures that delve into the latest trends in computer science, our department's events foster a collaborative and forward-thinking community. Join us on this digital journey as we celebrate the intersection of academia and technology, shaping the future of computing at the University of Ghana. Discover, engage, and be inspired by the limitless possibilities that unfold within the realm of computer science.
